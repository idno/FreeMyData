Social networks don't last forever.

Don't let your content get lost.

Download and save your social media data.

You publish content, photos, thoughts, and memories online every day. Don't throw it all away.

We created this directory so that you have an easy reference when you want to archive and backup your digital life.

The information below will help you export your data directly from the social network or website.

Twitter

Twitter will let you download an archive of all of your tweets, retweets, and Twitter images.

Twitter instructions

Log into your Twitter account. Navigate to Settings → Account → Content.

Next, click "Request your archive". You will be emailed by Twitter when the download is ready.

Facebook

Facebook will let you download a copy of your posts, photos, videos, messages, and more.

Facebook instructions

Log into your Facebook account. Navigate to Settings → General Account Settings.

At the bottom of the page is a link to "Download a copy of your Facebook data".

Foursquare

Foursquare makes feeds available for importing your check-in history into other platforms.

Foursquare instructions

Log into your Foursquare account, and navigate to the feeds page.

Choosing the ICS or GCAL formats will let you import your full check-in history into your calendar.

Choosing the RSS or KML formats will let you use the data with widgets and applications like Wordpress.

Evernote

With the desktop application for Evernote, you can download your Notes and Notebooks.

Evernote instructions

At this time, Evernote does not allow you to export your data from the web client.

On Mac:

Export a Note by selecting the note and choosing File → Export Note. Then select the format, either Evernote XML Format or HTML.

Export all notes in notebooks by selecting Notebook and choosing File → Export All Notebooks. Then select the format, either Evernote XML Format or HTML.

On Windows:

Export a Note by selecting the note and choosing File → Export Note. Then select the format, either Evernote XML Format or HTML.

Export a notebook by right-clicking on a Notebook and choosing File → Export Notebook. Then select the format, either Evernote XML Format or HTML.

Google+

Google will allow you to export Google+ information like your profile, stream, +1s, and circles.

Google+ instructions

Google Takeout provides exports of many Google services, including data associated with Google+. Once you're logged into your Google account and navigated to Takeout, you'll see that all products have been selected for download.

If you just want to download data associated with your Google+ account, select Profile, Hangouts, Google+ Circles, Google+ Stream, +1s, and Google Photos.

Select "Next" and then choose the file type and the delivery method (either email or to your Drive).

Known

Known will let you export all of your data, including posts, pictures, updates, and more.

Known instructions

Log into your Known website and go to Configure Your Site and then select Export Data.

Next click the "Start Exporting Your Data" link. When the export is done generating, the page will update, and you'll be able to download the link to all of your content.
